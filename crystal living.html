Style Manifesto: Living Light Design Ethos

Nature, Suspended in Crystal

The Hummingbird & Flower figurine captures a fleeting moment — wings mid-flutter, beak meeting blossom — and renders it permanent in faceted crystal. Soft jade-green wings, rose and amber petals, and a sapphire-blue head create a vivid, multi-tonal composition rarely seen in crystal figurines.

Sculptural Storytelling for the Home

Every facet is calculated to catch and scatter light differently depending on the hour and angle, so the piece feels alive on a shelf or console table. It is designed as a conversation piece first, a collectible second, and a permanent fixture of light in the home forever.

Signature Crystal Living Traits Multi-color faceted crystal body, sapphire-blue head accent, jade and rose-toned wing crystals, clear crystal stem and base, and a sculptural, museum-grade finish designed for display from every angle.

Storytelling Cue: Light as Architecture

"Imagine this piece not just on a shelf, but catching the morning sun. It does not just sit in the room — it redefines the light within it. Every wing facet was cut to scatter color the way real sunlight scatters through real wings in flight."

Rehearsal Prompt: The Space Integration

  • Staff Action: Place the piece in different lighting scenarios — near a window, under a warm spotlight, on a dark surface — and let the guest see how dramatically the color story shifts.
  • Engaging Question: Does this feel like the centerpiece of your dining room, or a delicate accent for your console table or vanity?
