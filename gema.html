Style Manifesto: Prismatic Collision Design Ethos

Asymmetry as Pure Joy

Gema is the collection that breaks the boundaries of standard pairing. Each mosaic is designed as an artistic clustering of distinct mixed cuts—pear, trilliant, and octagon—nestled edge-to-edge to mimic the glorious, chaotic order of crystals viewed through a prism.

Unexpected Color Collisions

By placing electric cobalt stones directly next to rich golden amber crystals, Gema creates a striking optical clash. This vivid pairing maximizes light dispersion, casting vibrant color-block shadows onto the skin.

Signature Gema Traits Asymmetric gem layouts, mixed-cut multi-colored crystal arrays, dynamic glass refraction overlays, and bold, chunky cocktail rings that celebrate self-expression.

Key Storytelling Cue: "Prismatic Play"

"Invite your guest to look at the asymmetric layout. Explain that Gema is about the joyful energy of unexpected colors coming together. It's designed to capture the way a white light beam breaks into a rainbow inside a pure glass prism."

Active Rehearsal Prompt: "The Refraction Shadow"

  • Staff Action: Shine a spotlight directly through Gema's cobalt and amber stones to throw colorful, contrasting shadow patterns on the surface.
  • Engaging Question: "This piece combines many geometric cuts instead of just one. Do you prefer the rich depth of the amber cushion cuts, or are you drawn to the electric glow of the cobalt trilliant?"
