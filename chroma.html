CHROMA Deconstructed Prisms & Saturated Geometry

Chroma reimagines crystals as pure, architectural building blocks. Bold, multi-cut gemstones are nestled inside vibrant, color-coated lacquered settings, maximizing optical depth and dispersing light like a modern, wearable rainbow.

Style Manifesto: Chroma Jewelry Storytelling Detail

The Rhythm of Saturated Gemstones

Traditional jewelry hides settings to showcase the stone. Chroma does the opposite: it celebrates settings. Gems are cradled in solid, lacquered metal frames that match the exact color frequency of the crystals, creating an illusion of double depth.

Modernism Over Convention

Instead of delicate, traditional links, the collection features bold, rigid chains and geometric shapes. This gives the jewelry a sculptural, modern silhouette that wears beautifully on skin or over clean tailoring.

Signature Chroma Design Traits Multi-colored cushion-cut crystals, color-matched lacquered metal frames, layered necklace formats, and structured gold-plated statement links — designed to stack light on skin.

Key Storytelling Cue: "Stained Glass on Skin"

"Invite your guest to hold the piece up to the light. Because the crystal settings are unbacked, they function like miniature stained-glass windows, casting saturated hues directly onto their skin."

Active Rehearsal Prompt: "The Color Play"

  • Staff Action: Place the Chroma piece over a plain blazer sleeve to show how color-coordinated settings pop instantly.
  • Engaging Question: "Does this combination of geometric cuts make you think of clean architectural lines, or do you see it as a bold splash of wearable pop art?"
