Style Manifesto: Angelic Radiance Design Ethos

Grace in Radiant Detail

Unlike traditional motifs, Una celebrates the organic curvature of the swan rendered in all-white pavé crystal. Each crystal is individually set to mirror the soft, fluid lines of a swan in motion — creating a piece that appears to emanate its own inner light rather than simply reflecting what surrounds it.

The Angelic Layered Composition

Una's defining campaign moment is the layered Angelic composition — three necklaces worn simultaneously, each length building on the previous, creating an overlapping field of white pavé brilliance that transforms the wearer into a radiant, transcendent presence.

Signature Una Traits All-white pavé crystal swan silhouette, high-prong refined settings for maximum light entry, rhodium-plated chain construction, sculpted contours for weightless wearability, and a signature multi-layer Angelic stacking system.

Storytelling Cue: Angelic Radiance

"Invite your guest to look at the profile of the Una swan. Point out that the pavé crystals catch light from every angle — it is like dressing in pure light, turning natural grace into a personal aura. The layered Angelic campaign shows the ultimate expression: three Una pieces worn together create a transcendent presence."

Rehearsal Prompt: The Grace Connect

  • How to present: Angle a Una piece toward the store spotlight and slowly rotate it. The all-white pavé creates a sweeping arc of light that demonstrates the full radiance potential. Ask: "Can you feel how it seems to generate its own light?"
  • Immersive Question: Do you prefer the sublime simplicity of a single Una piece, or does the layered Angelic composition — three necklaces worn as one — speak to your sense of occasion and personal radiance?
