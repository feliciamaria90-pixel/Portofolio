Style Manifesto: Plotted Sky Celestial Design

Connecting the Dots of Pure Light

Unlike traditional dense settings, Constella celebrates negative space. Each brilliant-cut stone floats along fine bar links, mapping out a celestial silhouette that mirrors the beautiful lines of night sky constellations.

The Elegant Floating Illusion

By minimizing the metal and raising the gems on elevated prongs, the stones catch light from 360 degrees. The necklace behaves like shimmering star droplets, resting weightlessly against the neck or skin.

Signature Constella Design Traits Round-cut clear crystals, Swarovski crystal pearls, claw prong settings, rhodium-plated fine chain links, and a choker silhouette designed for celestial layering.
