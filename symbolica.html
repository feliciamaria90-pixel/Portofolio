Style Manifesto: Wearable Intentions Design Ethos

Modern Talismans

Symbolica blends ancient protective symbology with a contemporary aesthetic. The Evil Eye pendant is designed as a wearable shield — its layered crystal architecture mimicking the anatomical structure of a real eye, transforming a millennia-old protective symbol into a collectible art object.

Layered Crystal Narratives

Each layer of the Evil Eye reveals a new dimension: the outer carved crystal disc for depth, the black pavé ring for protection, and the blue iris crystal for intention. This architectural layering rewards close inspection — always revealing something new to the wearer and observer alike.
