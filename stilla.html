Style Manifesto: Celestial Radiance Design Ethos

The Shape of Light

Stilla captures the luminous essence of the night sky in a round-cut white crystal pendant. The precision-cut facets refract light with unmatched clarity — creating an ever-shifting constellation of brilliance that mirrors the wonder of distant stars.

Celestial Precision

The rhodium-plated setting is deliberately understated — a cool, precise frame that lets the crystal command all attention. This balance of architectural restraint and brilliant centerpiece is what makes Stilla the perfect choice for both daily wear and meaningful gifting.
