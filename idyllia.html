IDYLLIA

Asymmetric Mixed Cuts & Color Collision

A celebration of spontaneous styling. The Idyllia Collection brings together a mosaic of geometric crystal cuts (octagon, cushion, trilliant) in unexpected color blockings—such as electric cobalt and golden amber—displayed through a glass prism setting.

Prismatic Display Concept
Style Manifesto: Prismatic Collision Design Ethos

Idyllia is the collection that breaks the boundaries of standard pairing. Each mosaic is designed as an artistic clustering of distinct mixed cuts—pear, trilliant, and octagon—nestled edge-to-edge to mimic the glorious, chaotic order of crystals viewed through a prism.

Asymmetry as Pure Joy

By placing electric cobalt stones directly next to rich golden amber crystals, Idyllia creates a striking optical clash. This vivid pairing maximizes light dispersion, casting vibrant color-block shadows onto the skin.

Signature Idyllia Traits Asymmetric gem layouts, mixed-cut multi-colored crystal arrays, dynamic glass refraction overlays, and bold, chunky cocktail rings that celebrate self-expression.
